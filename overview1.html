An overview

Taj Coromandel is one of Chennai's best known landmarks, suitable for both business and leisure travelers alike. The hotel is located near the airport, business district, and several popular venues. The Taj Coromandel is only six kilometers from the city center and nearby attractions include the Government House and the National Art Gallery. The much acclaimed and finest restaurants, Golden Dragon, Southern Spice, and Match Point, serves up immaculate traditional Indian and international cuisine. The business center is equipped with conference rooms and facilities necessary to host meetings and events. The swimming pool and fitness center are available for a little leisure and relaxation.
